Musical Accomplishments

  • Composed four full symphonies, including "Vozes da Amazônia," which premiered at the São Paulo Symphony Hall to critical acclaim
  • Created innovative fusion of classical composition techniques with indigenous Brazilian musical traditions
  • Performed as piano soloist with major orchestras including the Brazilian Symphony Orchestra and London Philharmonic
  • Released award-winning album "Raízes e Asas" (Roots and Wings) featuring collaborations with indigenous musicians
  • Youngest composer ever commissioned by the Brazilian Ministry of Culture for a national celebration
  • Mastered ten musical instruments, with primary focus on piano, classical guitar, and indigenous Brazilian percussion
  • Developed revolutionary teaching method combining classical music education with traditional Brazilian musical forms
  • Perfect score in Advanced Music Theory from the Royal Conservatory at age 14

Social Advocacy

  • Founded Música Para Todos (Music For All), providing free music education to over 10,000 children in underserved communities across Brazil
  • Developed curriculum for cultural preservation of indigenous musical traditions, now implemented in 50+ schools
  • Created documentary series "Sounds of Brazil" highlighting endangered musical traditions with 2 million online views
  • Led successful campaign to include traditional Brazilian music in the national educational curriculum
  • Organized benefit concerts raising over $500,000 for cultural preservation initiatives in the Amazon region
  • Appointed Youth Ambassador for UNESCO's Cultural Preservation Initiative at age 16

Academic Excellence

  • Pursuing dual degree in Music Composition and Social Anthropology at Harvard University on full scholarship
  • Published research on the neurological effects of traditional Brazilian rhythms in the Journal of Ethnomusicology
  • Graduated top of class from São Paulo Conservatory of Music at age 15
  • Fluent in Portuguese, English, Spanish, and three indigenous Brazilian languages
  • Developed innovative research methodology for documenting and preserving endangered musical traditions
  • Presented TED Talk "Music as Cultural Memory" with over 5 million views

Current Projects

Symphony of the Amazon

Digital Archive of Brazilian Musical Traditions

Developing an interactive digital platform to document, preserve, and teach traditional Brazilian musical forms, with special focus on indigenous and endangered traditions across different regions.

Educational Access Initiative
