Medical Devices

Medical Innovations

  • Invented PulsePoint, an affordable handheld diagnostic device that performs 12 critical health screenings using a single drop of blood, accessible via smartphone integration
  • Developed a solar-powered portable dialysis system that operates without consistent electricity, now deployed in 27 rural clinics across Southeast Asia
  • Created AI-based diagnostic algorithm that can detect early signs of retinopathy from smartphone photos with 94% accuracy, published in New England Journal of Medicine
  • Designed low-cost prosthetic limbs with advanced myoelectric control systems, reducing production costs by 85% while maintaining functionality
  • Pioneered 3D-printable medical tools and devices specifically designed for resource-limited settings, now used in over 200 clinics globally
  • Developed innovative telemedicine platform connecting remote patients with specialists, handling over 50,000 consultations annually
  • Created biodegradable surgical implants that reduce rejection risk and eliminate need for removal surgeries
  • Recipient of the prestigious WHO Innovation in Healthcare Award 2024

Field Implementation

  • Founded MediTech For All, a non-profit organization deploying innovative medical technologies in 18 countries across Asia, Africa, and South America
  • Established mobile diagnostic labs serving over 100,000 patients annually in rural China, providing access to advanced medical tests previously unavailable
  • Trained more than 2,000 community health workers in using simple diagnostic tools and telemedicine systems
  • Created network of 3D printing hubs for on-demand production of medical supplies in remote locations
  • Developed and implemented remote patient monitoring systems for managing chronic conditions in areas with physician shortages
  • Partnered with 35 hospitals to implement AI-assisted diagnostic tools, reducing diagnostic errors by 42%

Academic & Research Background

  • Ph.D. in Biomedical Engineering, Stanford University, with focus on point-of-care diagnostics
  • M.S. in Electrical Engineering, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, specializing in medical device development
  • B.S. in Bioengineering, Tsinghua University, graduated summa cum laude
  • Research Fellowship at the World Health Organization's Innovation Hub, developing healthcare solutions for low-resource environments
  • Visiting Scientist at Johns Hopkins Center for Bioengineering Innovation & Design, where she developed the initial PulsePoint prototype
  • Author of "Democratizing Medical Diagnostics," which has become a foundational text in the field of global health technology
