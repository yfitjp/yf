Academic Excellence

Research Projects

  • Collaborating with IBM Quantum research team on next-generation quantum computing architecture
  • Principal investigator on a GEF-funded project exploring the application of quantum algorithms to protein folding problems, with potential implications for drug discovery

Future Research Goals

Elena is currently focused on developing practical quantum algorithms that can address real-world problems in climate science, materials engineering, and pharmaceutical research. Her long-term research vision includes:

  • Creating fault-tolerant quantum computing systems that can maintain quantum coherence for extended periods
  • Developing quantum networks that enable secure communication and distributed quantum computing across global distances
  • Applying quantum computing to solve previously intractable problems in molecular simulation, potentially revolutionizing drug discovery
  • Building educational frameworks to make quantum computing concepts accessible to broader audiences, fostering the next generation of quantum scientists
