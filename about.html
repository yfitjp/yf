About Our Foundation

Excellence Through Global Innovation and Leadership

Our Mission

Global Excellence Foundation (GEF) is dedicated to identifying, supporting, and nurturing exceptional young talent across the globe. We believe in creating opportunities for extraordinary individuals to reach their full potential and make meaningful contributions to society.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between exceptional ability and exceptional opportunity, ensuring that the most promising minds of each generation can access the resources, mentorship, and platforms they need to excel.

Our History

Founded in 2015 by a coalition of educators, industry leaders, and philanthropists, GEF began with a simple premise: extraordinary talent exists in every corner of the world, but opportunity does not.

What started as a scholarship program supporting 10 exceptional students has grown into a comprehensive global initiative with multiple programs spanning academic excellence, entrepreneurship, leadership development, and social impact projects.

Today, our network includes over 500 scholars across 45 countries, with alumni who have gone on to found successful companies, lead groundbreaking research, and create positive change in their communities.

Global Reach

With headquarters in Tokyo and regional offices in New York, London, Singapore, and Cape Town, GEF operates globally to ensure diverse representation and maximize our impact.

Our scholars come from every continent, representing over 45 countries and speaking more than 60 languages. This global perspective enriches our community and strengthens our collective ability to address complex global challenges.
